Water Project

The water project in Kisii was built to address the community's water needs. It provides clean, potable water, improving health and sanitation while reducing the burden of water collection. This boosts the overall quality of life and stimulates economic growth through agricultural support. Gender equality is promoted as women and girls benefit from reduced water-fetching responsibilities. The project also aligns with global sustainable development goals. It was constructed due to water scarcity, health concerns, and the goal of sustainable community development. In sum, it enhances well-being, fosters economic development, and ensures a better future for Kisii residents.
